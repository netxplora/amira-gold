# Amira Gold User Manual

Welcome to Amira Gold. This guide provides instructions on how to use our platform to purchase jewelry, trade digital gold, and manage your secure storage.

## 1. Getting Started

### Account Creation
1. Navigate to the Amira Gold homepage.
2. Click **Get Started** or **Login**.
3. Follow the authentication prompts to create your account using your email.

### Profile & KYC
To access all features, including high-value purchases and vault storage, you must complete the Identity Verification (KYC) process:
1. Go to your **Profile Settings**.
2. Upload a valid government-issued ID and a clear selfie.
3. Provide your current residential address.
4. Our team will review your submission within 24–48 hours.

## 2. Jewelry Marketplace

### Browsing and Filtering
*   **Categories**: Filter jewelry by type (e.g., Rings, Necklaces, Bracelets).
*   **Purity**: Choose between 18k, 22k, or 24k gold.
*   **Live Pricing**: All jewelry prices are updated in real-time based on the global gold market rate.

### Purchasing
1. Add items to your **Cart**.
2. Proceed to **Checkout**.
3. Provide your delivery address and select a courier.
4. Choose a payment method:
    *   **Wallet Balance**: Pay instantly using funds already in your Amira Gold account.
    *   **Digital Currency**: Send payment to the provided address and upload your proof of payment.

## 3. Digital Gold & Vaulting

### Buying Digital Gold
*   Digital gold allows you to own gold in fractional amounts (starting from $10).
*   Navigate to the **Invest** section to see live buy/sell rates.
*   Purchased digital gold is instantly credited to your holdings.

### Vault Storage
*   You can choose to store physical gold bars in our secure global vaults (Zurich, Dubai, London, etc.).
*   View your stored assets and their current market value in the **Reserves** dashboard.

## 4. Financial Management

### Wallet
*   **Top-up**: Add funds to your wallet via supported payment methods.
*   **Withdrawal**: Request to withdraw your balance to your external accounts.
*   **Transaction History**: View a detailed log of all your purchases, sales, and transfers.

## 5. Support

### Support Tickets
If you encounter any issues:
1. Go to the **Support** section in your dashboard.
2. Create a new ticket with a clear subject and description.
3. You can attach images or documents to help our team resolve your query.

### Real-time Chat
For quick questions, use the integrated chat widget located at the bottom right of the screen to speak with an agent.
